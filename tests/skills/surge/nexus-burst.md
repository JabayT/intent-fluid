# PRD Draft: Project "Nexus-Burst" (Omni-Channel Brand Breakthrough)

## Background

Our flagship product (an AI-driven social or productivity tool) has reached a growth plateau. We need an explosive marketing campaign to revitalize the brand using the latest tech trends and achieve a "viral" status across social media platforms.

## Core Objectives

Rebranding: Enhance the "Cool Factor" and "Intelligence Image" of the brand among Gen Z and professional demographics.

Omni-Channel Penetration: The campaign must cover mainstream social platforms with a matrix-based distribution strategy.

User Acquisition: Drive significant traffic conversion and increase Daily Active Users (DAU).

## Preliminary Concepts

AI Interaction: Incorporate an engaging AI-powered mechanic (e.g., generative art, voice cloning, or digital avatars).

Emotional Resonance: The narrative should spark social discussion and encourage organic sharing.

Cross-Over Collaboration: Consider partnering with influencers or brands from diverse sectors.

## Deliverables

A comprehensive campaign timeline.

Drafts of content distribution strategies for multiple platforms.

A projected growth and engagement data model.

## Budget & Resources

Budget is flexible, depending on the creativity and impact of the proposal.

Internal technical resources are available for minor development needs.
